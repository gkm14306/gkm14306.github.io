<H1>Guide to Exploring Houston, Texas</H1>

<p class="dotted"> I was born and raised in Houston, Texas. Until I moved to a different city, I never appreciated all that my city had to offer. There are so many things that I miss about Houston and wish that my firends could experience. First, I miss the food. As much as I love Athens' southern cooking, I find myself always craving tex-mex. I also miss all the fun things I could do in Houston.  As one of the largest cities in the United States, Houston has many attractions and activities to explore! Allow me to explain to you why I think everyone should come and see my hometown, Houston, Texas. </p>
